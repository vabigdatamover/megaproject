Mission

The purpose of this project was to analyze how Earthquakes affects us. To accomplish this analysis, we first pulled data from numerous sources such as the US Geological Survey, news sites around the world and a 44 year trove of earthquake activity.


After assembling the dataset, D3 and Leaflet was used to plot earthquakes worldwide with infrastructure and hazardous correlation layers. This has the added benefit of functionality to animate all fourty four years of timeline. All fourty four years of worldwide earthquake data is searchable to vistors of the website. Additionally, some of the most popular earthquake information is web scrapable upon demand by user with the click of a button. Future interations of this website will include machine learning data which will try to predict future earthquakes based upon previous earthquake data.
